The Product

Altitude-grown.

curcumin content.

Peruvian turmeric is cultivated in Peru's highland growing regions, where cool nights and mineral-rich soils produce roots with exceptional curcumin concentration and a clean, earthy aroma profile. For food formulators and nutraceutical manufacturers, consistency is the product — and Good Hope Produce delivers it at scale.

Available as fresh whole root, dried slices, and dried whole root. Organic and conventional grades. Export packaging to EU, US, and Canadian import standards.

Procurement teams sourcing turmeric at volume face one consistent problem: variability. Curcumin levels fluctuate by region and season, and documentation gaps at origin create compliance exposure in regulated markets. Our certified supply chain is designed to eliminate both.

Our certified supply chain runs from verified highland farms through Global G.A.P.-compliant post-harvest handling to export-ready container loading — with full traceability documentation at every step.
